Kingdom Rush
UNITY C Sharp
Kingdom Rush is a 3D tower defense game where enemies attempt to reach the kingdom to loot gold. Players must strategically place towers to stop them before they escape with the treasure.
Programming Features
  • Object Pooling: Optimized enemy instantiation by reusing destroyed enemy objects to improve performance.
  • Enemy Pathfinding: Implemented Breadth-First Search (BFS) for determining the shortest path to the castle. Used Vector3.Lerp() for smooth enemy movement along the path.
  • Currency System: Designed a Gold System to manage the player's money for tower placement and penalties when enemies reach the castle.
  • Enemy Health System: Added a health mechanism for enemies, reducing health upon tower attacks and destroying enemies when health reaches zero.
  • Shooting Mechanism: Towers shoot projectiles at enemies, dealing damage based on the enemy's health, with shooting intervals managed through coroutines.
  • Event System: Triggered events for key actions like: 1. Enemy reaching the castle (reducing currency). 2. Player placing towers (deducting currency).
