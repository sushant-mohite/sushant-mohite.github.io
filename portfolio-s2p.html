My work at S2P Edu

Some of the simulators created for training that I worked on.

Measurements of physical quantities

This simulator displays practical examples of measurements of physical quantities such as mass, time and length.

Math Exercises

This simulator allows students to practice base operations. It is possible to select which operation to play and add a timer for extra challenge.

Fire Fighting Training

This simulator aims to teach about the various types of fire extinguishers and how to check for signs of wear and damage.

Newton's Second Law

This simulator aims to teach about the Newton's Second Law of Motion while giving a more interactive ambient to see it in action.

Electrical conductivity in water

This simulator aims to teach about how different substances affects electrical conductivity in water in a practical environment.

Underground Chamber Maintenance

This simulator is used for the training of underground chamber maintenance, checking for the best practices for work safety and the steps necessary to complete the task.
