Rocket Boost
UNITY C Sharp
Rocket Boost is a physics-based 2.5D game where players control a rocket by managing its thrust and rotation. The objective is to navigate through challenging obstacles and land on landing pad.
Programming Features
  • Input Handling: Controlled rocket movement using player inputs for thrust and rotation.
  • Physics & Collision Detection: Applied force and gravity to create realistic rocket movement and flight dynamics.
  • Particle System: Added thrust effects and explosions to enhance visual feedback.
  • Level Design: Created challenging levels with varying obstacles and landing pads to test player control.
  • Scene Management: Handled transitions between levels and restart scenarios seamlessly.
  • Skybox & Lighting: Designed immersive visuals with a custom skybox and optimized lighting.
